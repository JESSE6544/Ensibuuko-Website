Our Digital Financial Solutions

Explore our suite of tools designed to digitize and empower SACCOs, VSLAs, MFIs and communities across Africa.

Chomoka

Digitizing Village Savings Groups

Chomoka transforms traditional VSLA record keeping with a mobile-first system. Record meetings, track contributions, issue loans, automate calculations and integrate Mobile Money.

MOBIS

Complete Digital Banking for SACCOs & MFIs

MOBIS is a comprehensive core banking suite built for rural financial institutions. Manage accounts, automate reporting, enable mobile & USSD member access, and improve operational efficiency.

Lending Services

Accessible Financing for Underserved Communities

Through strong partnerships, Ensibuuko offers agro-loans, emergency loans, and working capital financing tailored to rural communities and micro‑enterprises.
