Who we are

We are more than a software company. We are a team of passionate engineers and professionals that are deeply committed to solving the issues holding back financial inclusion in Africa.

We are deploying technology solutions to community-based savings and loans organisations so they can efficiently reach and serve unbanked and most underserved communities in Africa with affordable and relevant financial services. Our proprietary microfinance solutions help these organizations to automate data, process and payments and to become efficient and bankable.

Our vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to relevant and affordable financial products and services; and possesses the knowledge needed to make sound decisions about using them.

Our mission

Our mission is to drive financial inclusion by growing the developing world's digital financial infrastructure.

We do this by using our expertise to design and implement digital products and services that connect the unbanked to the wider financial ecosystem.
