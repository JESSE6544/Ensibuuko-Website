Empowering Financial Inclusion in Uganda.

We design and implement user-friendly digital products and services that connect unbanked and underserved communities to formal financial systems, enabling them to access, use, and benefit from financial services in meaningful ways.

Bridging the Gap for Underserved Communities

At Ensibuuko, we’re revolutionizing financial access in rural Uganda by delivering innovative fintech solutions that empower financial institutions, SACCOs, and village savings groups (VSLAs). Our mission is simple: to drive financial inclusion by building and expanding digital financial infrastructure across the developing world through technology that’s accessible, reliable, and impactful.

Chomoka

Digitizing Village Savings Groups

Empower your VSLA with Chomoka, our mobile app that brings transparency and efficiency to group activities. Track contributions, record meetings, send reminders, manage loans, penalties, and integrate mobile money — all in one place.

MOBIS

The All‑in‑One Rural Banking SaaS Platform

Transform operations with MOBIS, our suite for SACCOs and MFIs. Manage accounts seamlessly via our web app, while members access services through mobile apps or USSD.
