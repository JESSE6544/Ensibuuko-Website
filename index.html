SACCOs

Digitally Empowering Member-Owned Cooperatives

Savings and Credit Cooperative Societies (SACCOs) are democratic, member-owned institutions that mobilize savings and offer credit. We help SACCOs grow stronger by digitizing operations, improving transparency, and expanding access to financial services.

Lending Companies

A New Way of Doing Lending — Digitally

Lending companies provide micro and medium-sized loans to individuals and businesses. We introduce these institutions to secure, scalable digital platforms that streamline loan management, reporting, and customer engagement.

Savings Groups

Secure and Prosperous Community Savings

Savings Groups, also known as Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLAs), are community-based financial circles. We help these groups transition from cash-based systems to secure, cashless, and paperless digital tools that promote accountability and growth.
